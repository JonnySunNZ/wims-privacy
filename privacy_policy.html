Privacy Policy for WIMS

Effective Date: 2025-05-20

This Privacy Policy describes how WIMS ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Microphone Access

We use your device's microphone to allow you to record voice notes about item locations. These recordings are analyzed and saved locally on your device.

2. Camera Access

You can use your device’s camera to take photos of items or their storage locations. These images are saved locally and may be processed via Google Cloud Vision API for visual analysis.

3. Local Storage

All data, including photos and audio, is stored locally in your device’s internal database. We do not access or upload your data to any servers.

4. Cloud Processing

While data is stored locally, user-uploaded photos may be sent to Google Cloud Vision API for analysis. These photos are not stored on our servers.

5. Location Data

WIMS does not collect or use your device’s location data.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. These third-party services may collect anonymous usage data as per their own privacy policies.

7. Account and Login

No login or account creation is required to use WIMS.

8. Future Cloud Storage

Currently, we do not upload any data to the cloud. However, we may introduce cloud features in the future. This policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: yi.sun@outlook.co.nz
