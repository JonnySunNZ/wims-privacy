Privacy Policy for WIMS
    Effective Date: 2025-05-20
    
    WIMS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information. By using WIMS, you agree to the terms of this policy.
    
    1. Information We Collect
    WIMS may collect the following information from your device:
    
    Microphone Access: Used solely to allow users to record voice input for storing item locations. These recordings are processed and saved locally on your device.
    
    Camera Access: Used to capture images of items or their storage locations, at the user's discretion.
    
    Local Storage: All recorded audio, captured photos, and related metadata are saved only on your device in a local database.
    
    No Location Data: WIMS does not collect or store any geolocation data.
    
    2. Cloud Processing (Limited)
    Images captured by users may be temporarily sent to Google Cloud Vision API to analyze and assist in object recognition.
    
    No personal identifiers are attached to these images.
    
    The app does not store this data on any cloud storage or external server.
    
    3. Third-Party Services
    WIMS uses the following third-party services:
    
    Google AdMob: To display in-app advertisements.
    
    Google Cloud Vision API: To analyze uploaded images for object recognition.
    
    These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their respective policies:
    
    Google AdMob Privacy Policy
    
    Google Cloud Privacy Notice
    
    4. No Account or Login Required
    WIMS does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
    
    5. Data Security
    All data (images, voice input, item details) is stored securely on your local device.
    
    We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.
    
    6. Future Changes
    In the future, WIMS may introduce cloud sync features. Any such feature will be opt-in and explained clearly before activation.
    
    7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective upon publication.
